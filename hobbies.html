    How to make a PB&J Sandwich ⟡𓌉◯𓇋₊˚♡

    Ingredients

    • 2 slices of bread
    • Peanut Butter
    • Jelly
    • Plate

    Directions

    1. Spread Peanut Butter on one slice of bread with a butter knife.
    2. Spread the other slice of bread with jelly using the same butter knife.
    3. Sandwich the two slices of bread together with the spread side in the inside.
    4. Place sandwich on plate and slice the sandwich in half.
    5. Enjoy!
